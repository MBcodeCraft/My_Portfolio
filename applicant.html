All Benefits ID Information

PhilHealth ID

This ID provides proof of membership in the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth), allowing access to health insurance benefits under the National Health Insurance Program.

TIN (Tax Identification Number)

The TIN is issued by the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) to all taxpayers. It is required for employment and financial transactions related to tax.

Pag-IBIG ID

The Pag-IBIG ID provides access to housing loans and savings benefits through the Home Development Mutual Fund (HDMF).

PSA (Philippine Statistics Authority)

The PSA ID, also known as the Philippine National ID, serves as a unified, government-recognized identification for all citizens and resident aliens in the Philippines.

SSS ID (Social Security System)

The SSS ID provides private sector employees with access to social insurance programs including retirement, disability, and death benefits.

QC ID (Quezon City ID)

This ID is issued to residents of Quezon City and provides access to various city-specific programs and services.

Driver's License

This ID is issued by the Land Transportation Office (LTO) and serves as a legal proof of the right to drive within the country.
